Top Destinations in London

By London Guide Recommendation

1

Greenwich

This is a district in the south of London, one of London's richest places, just 8 minutes from downtown London by Southeastern bullet train.

2

Warner Bros. Studio Tour London

If Singapore is famous for Universal Studios, London is a desirable destination for Potterheads - fans of the famous Harry Potter series.

3

Tower of London

The Queen's Palace and Fortress, also commonly known as the Tower of London, is a historic site located in the heart of the city, on the north bank of the River Thames.

4

Madame Tussaud's Wax Museum

Madame Tussauds is one of the famous tourist destinations in the UK, where more than 300 wax statues of famous people are displayed.

5

Buckingham Palace

Buckingham Palace is Britain's most famous residence, Queen Elizabeth II's luxurious residence and workplace.

6

Museums and art galleries

Like France and most other European countries, the UK has many great museums and art galleries.
